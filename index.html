Biography and Goals

I was born and raised in the small island of Guam on 1985.
Throughout the years I have been exposed to a laid back
family-oriented lifestyle.

My parents moved from Korea post Vietnam War and started up a
construction company as the opportunity presented itself due
to the military build up at the time. They raised three kids (myself
included), two boys and one girl.

In 2003 I moved to Seattle to attend the University of Washington for
my bachelors. And then back to Guam in 2008 after the recession hit and
was unable to find a job. In 2012 I moved to Hawaii for opportunity, but
while the sun and beaches were fun to enjoy I found my life stagnating.
That is why I decided to join Code Fellows for an opportunity to pursue
a meaningful life time career.

My goals are to become a top-notch coder through the help of Code
Fellows and be able to make my folks proud of the career I've developed.
